    My name is Jolanta [yo-LAN-ta]. I am a product designer based in Barcelona focused on solving problems through evidence-based, user-centered design.

    I started my design career by joining early-stage startups in Spain and Denmark, often as the only designer. I spent the last seven years working at tech companies in Silicon Valley.

    I have expertise in design systems and have been working with multiple product teams to ensure consistent and delightful experiences.

    In 2021, I led the newly founded Instagram's accessibility team, where my multidisciplinary background came full circle, uniting my love for research, driving community-wide initiatives, and education, as well as my personal commitment to design for good.

    I am now leading Instagram's Business Messaging design system and building solutions that help businesses grow and develop personal relationships with their customers through Meta's messaging products.

    When I am not pixel-pushing and leading product initiatives, you will find me traveling the world, learning foreign languages, mentoring, or working on my side projects.
