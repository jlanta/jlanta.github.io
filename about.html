My name is Jolanta [yo-LAN-ta]. I am a product designer based in Barcelona focused on solving problems through evidence-based, user-centered design.

I started my design career by joining early-stage startups in Spain and Denmark, often as the only designer. I spent the last seven years working at tech companies in Silicon Valley.

In 2019, I joined a tiny but mighty Instgram's Design Systems team and led Instagram's design component library creation, design system documentation, and tooling initiatives.

In 2021, I led the newly founded Instagram's accessibility team, where my multidisciplinary background came full circle; uniting my love for research, creating educational and training material, coaching and mentoring, as well as my personal commitment to design for good.

Now, I am leading Instagram's Business Messaging design system, helping businesses connect with their customers. My responsibilities include increasing user experience quality across Instagram and all of Meta's messaging apps.
