    My name is Jolanta [yo-LAN-ta]. I am a product designer based in Barcelona focused on solving problems through evidence-based, user-centered design.

    I have expertise in design systems and have been working with multiple product teams to deliver consistent and delightful experiences. I contributed to Instagram's design system and empowered cross-functional teams to use it by developing internal tools and providing support in feedback groups and during office hours.

    Now, I am leading Instagram's Business Messaging design system and building solutions that help businesses grow and develop personal relationships with their customers through Meta's messaging products.

    Before I led the newly founded Instagram's accessibility team, where my multidisciplinary background came full circle; uniting my love for research, driving community-wide initiatives and education, and my personal commitment to design for good.

    Before working for tech companies in Silicon Valley, I worked at early-stage startups in Spain and Denmark, often as the only designer.

    When I am not pixel-pushing and leading product initiatives, you will find me traveling the world, attending film festivals, learning foreign languages, mentoring, or working on personal side projects.
