Playfulbet

ROLE: Graphic designer
RESPONSIBILITIES: Illustrations for a website, marketing materials design

Playfulbet is a start-up founded in 2012 in Barcelona, Spain. It is a social sports betting game where users use virtual coins, guess sports results and challenge their friends.
I joined this start-up in summer of 2013 and got a great opportunity to "play with pixels". The design was based on 8-bit style. As a graphic designer, I was preparing all kind of graphic materials: illustrations, badges.
At the time I was working in Playfulbet the team was changing the design and trying to make it more user-friendly. My task was to keep it's identity-based on 8-bit by providing illustrations.

Every player get badges:

One of the most interesting parts of work in this project was to prepare 8bit characters representing famous sports people.
I have also prepared theme illustrations for a home page, social networks, newsletters.
