    Playfulbet

    Visual style and growth design for social sports betting game

    Introduction

    Playfulbet, a social sports betting game, was founded in 2011 in Barcelona. The game allowed its users to accumulate virtual coins and exchange them for both physical and digital prizes. It was acquired by Exogroup in 2018.

    My role

    In 2013, I joined Playfulbet as its sole designer. I supported the team of three engineers and was responsible for its visual style. During my time at Playfulbet, one of the main goals for design was to redesign it to a more clean aesthetics while keeping its unique 8-bit style.

    My daily tasks also included sales and marketing support. I created a high volume of illustrations for lead generation campaigns, newsletters, display ads, and other sales and marketing collateral, instructional videos, and animations.

    Visual style

    Playbulfet was aimed mainly at the male audience between 18 and 35 years old and had its largest market in Spain. To differentiate from the existing competitors and deliver a more unique and relatable product, the chosen visual style was playful and resembled simple, decidedly retro, gameplay. This style was incorporated into communication design for growth-driven marketing campaigns as well as the product's UI.

    Focus

    Playfulbet's mission was to connect people and build a strong community. At the early stage of the product, the main focus was on user growth. In addition to targeted marketing campaigns, new features that increase engagement were added: challenge friends, and raise user level.     Result

    After the redesign and introduction of more gamification elements, Playfulbet grew from 80,000 users to more than 2 million registered users and 10 million monthly pageviews.
