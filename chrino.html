    Chrino Studio

    Chrino Studio was founded in Copenhagen (Denmark) and its first collection "MINIMALISM" was launched in April 2016. I was the first visual designer on the team and was working closely with company's founders to develop brand and visual style. My responsibilities included: visual identity, branding, layout for daily content and content marketing material.

    The company creates children clothing which is comfortable, versatile and most importantly high quality. The brand stands for minimalist lifestyle, no waste and responsible consuming. Chrino's visual identity was based on the same principles: simple and minimal.
