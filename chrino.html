CHRINO STUDIO

As a graphic deisgner and marketing assistant I was working closely with its founders. My responsabilities were: visual identity, layout for daily content and content marketing material.
CHRINO Studio creates children clothing which is confortable, vertasile and most important high quality. The brand stands for minimalist lifestyle, no waste and responsible consuming. CHRINO's visual identity was based on the same principles: simple and minimal.
